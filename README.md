# 🛒 Online Retail Sales Analysis (SQL + Excel Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data of an online retail store to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, customer behavior, and top-performing products.

The goal is to demonstrate **data analysis skills using SQL and Excel**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total revenue performance
* Identify top-selling products
* Find high-value customers
* Understand category and city performance
* Track monthly sales trends

---

## 🧱 Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following columns:

* `order_id` – Unique order ID
* `customer_name` – Customer name
* `product` – Product purchased
* `category` – Product category
* `quantity` – Units purchased
* `price` – Price per unit
* `order_date` – Date of purchase
* `city` – Customer location

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* SQL (MySQL)
* Microsoft Excel (Charts & Dashboard)

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Total Revenue

Calculated overall business revenue using SQL aggregation.

### 2. Revenue by Category

Identified which product categories generate the most revenue.

### 3. Top Selling Products

Analyzed most demanded products based on quantity sold.

### 4. Customer Analysis

* Identified high-spending customers
* Found repeat customers

### 5. City-wise Performance

Compared revenue across different cities.

### 6. Monthly Sales Trend

Tracked revenue trends over time.

---

## 📈 Dashboard

The Excel dashboard includes:

* Bar chart (Revenue by Category)
* Pie chart (Category Contribution)
* Line chart (Monthly Trend)

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Clothing category generated the highest revenue
* A small number of customers contributed to most sales
* Repeat customers indicate good retention
* Delhi and Mumbai are top-performing cities
* Sales trends show potential for seasonal offers

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing on top-performing categories
* Introduce loyalty programs for repeat customers
* Increase inventory for best-selling products
* Target high-performing cities with ads
